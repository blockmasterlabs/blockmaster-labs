03 · Protocol

How it works.

Four steps. Permissionless. Non-custodial. No middleman holds your funds.

01

Deposit

Connect a wallet. Choose vault. Deposit any amount. Funds held in audited PoolTogether V5 prize vaults. Withdraw 100% any time.

02

Earn yield

Your deposit auto-routes to Aave V3 to generate variable yield. The yield is pooled across all depositors — not paid out individually.

03

Random draw

PoolTogether V5's native RNG runs periodic draws. Pooled yield distributes as prizes. Larger deposit · longer time · higher odds.

04

Withdraw

Principal is never at risk from the protocol mechanism. Withdraw any time. No lockup. No penalty. Your funds, your keys.

05 · Risk

What can go wrong.

We don't sell certainty. Read this before you deposit. Then read it again.

R-01

"No-loss" is not absolute

Refers to yield-bearing principal preservation under normal protocol conditions. Does not mean your deposit is risk-free.

R-02

Smart contract risk

PoolTogether V5, Aave V3, and chain contracts are audited but no audit eliminates risk. Bugs and exploits are possible.

R-03

Issuer freeze risk

USDC (Circle) and USDT (Tether) can freeze wallets at the issuer level under legal order. DAI has no central issuer — exempt.

R-04

Stablecoin depeg

Stablecoins can deviate from $1. USDC, USDT, and DAI have each depegged in the past. Recovery is not guaranteed.

R-05

Not a security · Not advice

LetsGetIt is a non-custodial interface to a permissionless protocol. Nothing here is investment, tax, or legal advice.

R-06

You are responsible

You hold the keys. You make the deposits. You choose the vault. We give you tools and disclosures. The decisions are yours.
